Who We Are

Welcome to Seedling Theatre Company! We are a performing arts studio serving the Henderson, Southern Las Vegas, and Boulder City areas. We offer a variety of courses for kids aged 4 to 18 years. Our studio features a fully equipped theatre (stage, lighting, sound), along with additional rooms for dance and vocal performance classes. Stay connected with us for regular updates by subscribing to our email list below.

Our Mission

Seedling Theatre Company is dedicated to providing an environment where children and teens feel welcomed, encouraged, and empowered through participating in the performing arts. Our instructors teach with patience and love, striving to develop the potential in every student.
